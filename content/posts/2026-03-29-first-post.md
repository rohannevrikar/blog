This is the first post on my new blog. I had another one hosted on Wordpress but realized it's just an overhead at this point as I haven't been writing anything actively. Didn't feel the need of it to be honest until now. 

I have been fortunate to get access to the best AI tools and resources quite early in this AI boom, because of which my day to day ways of working have evolved quite significantly (every other software developer's story I guess). Most of the things have improved work-wise. I'm happy about productivity at an individual level, and how this transformation is able to help me connect with domain-experts to help them solve their problems. However, there's a rather funny cost to working like this. Not working on multiple things at once = Am I not doing enough? Not finishing up a greenfield project within a day = Was my Claude setup not good enough? 

With all these retrospective thoughts, one thought stood with me- Try to sit down for a couple of minutes everyday and produce something original without using Claude. Writing about my day, recent learnings, frustrations, etc sounds original. Hence, this blog. 

I hope to remain consistent at writing. Let's see how this goes.
